About


Can we ever solve the problem of World Hunger?
The Food Direction Challenge is an ambitious attempt to solve
the problem of World Hunger.
Smart farming and agricultural use of land globally could
be the answer to eliminating world hunger.

In our project we look at the data trends that emerge and try
to create a way to visualize food direction across the globe.
For bulk of the project we graphed employment in agriculture,
cereal yields, and population growth over the past few years.

We are hoping that the findings in the data collected will help
solving the problems of world hunger. A lot of the data found
show that agriculture does produce enough crops to sustain the
current human population.
